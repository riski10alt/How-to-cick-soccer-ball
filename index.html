How to correctly kick a soccer ball

Step 1

Approach the ball from an angle, with your non-kicking foot slightly behind and to the side of the ball.

Step 2

Plant your foot beside the ball, with your toes pointing towards your target.

Step 3

Swing your kicking leg back and then forward in a smooth motion, keeping your knee slightly bent and your foot pointed down.

Step 4

Make contact with the ball with the laces or instep of your foot, aiming to hit the center of the ball.

Step 5

Follow through with your kicking leg, allowing it to continue forward after making contact with the ball.
